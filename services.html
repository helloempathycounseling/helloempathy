Services

Support for adults and minors, delivered with empathy and clarity.

Individual Therapy

One‑on‑one sessions for adults or minors to address anxiety, addiction, life transitions, and overall mental health wellness.

Group Therapy

Skills‑based and process groups that foster connection, learning, and accountability in a supportive setting.

Family Support

Guidance for caregivers and families to improve communication and reinforce healthy patterns at home.

  • Anxiety & stress management
  • Self‑esteem and identity development
  • School and social challenges (minors)
  • Career and life transitions

Approaches

  • Adlerian — practical, goal‑oriented, and encouragement‑focused

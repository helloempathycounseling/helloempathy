Meet Namika Mahmoodi, LCPC

Rehabilitation Specialist for Minors and Psychotherapist. Namika Zaman Mahmoodi earned her Masters and Post‑Masters in Clinical Psychology at Johns Hopkins University and is a Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor (LCPC). She completed her undergraduate degree at the University of Maryland, College Park.

She has provided individual and group therapy in Baltimore City and County, supporting adults and minors with addiction, mental health, and anxiety concerns. Namika also spent four years with the Baltimore Alliance for Careers in Healthcare, offering career readiness and workforce support to immigrants and city residents.

Currently, she serves as a community therapist at Created for Greater, working with adults and minors in individual and group formats. Her style is empathic and collaborative, placing unconditional positive regard at the forefront.
